Instructions

In this experiment, you will be shown videos of creatures from planet Gaya interacting with one another.

On planet Gaya there are two types of creatures: Blues and Greens. While planet Gaya is mostly safe, it features red fireballs that only Blues can see. Greens cannot see the red fireballs! Fireballs are dangerous for Greens but not for Blues. Blues have a thicker skin that protects them from being burned. Fireballs are affected by magnetic winds which cause them to move.

Blues and Greens get a long well most of the time. However, there have been some instances in which a Blue was involved and a Green was burned by colliding with a red fireball.

In the following, we will show you videos of situations in which a Green was burned by colliding with a fireball. For each video, you will judge how much Blue is to blame for Green getting burned.

Before beginning with the experiment, we will tell you a little more about planet Gaya, the Blues, Greens, and fireballs.
